# Molido AI Business Rules

## Product Rules

- Every product has:
  - Purchase Price
  - Sale Price
  - Expiration Date
  - Barcode
  - Stock

## Invoice Rules

- Every invoice must have:
  - Customer/Supplier
  - Items
  - Quantity
  - Price
  - Date

## Inventory Rules

- Stock updates automatically after sale/purchase
- Low stock creates notification
- Expiration creates alert

## Accounting Rules

- Sales create income records
- Purchases create expense records
- Payments update balances

## AI Rules

AI can:
- Analyze sales
- Predict demand
- Suggest pricing
- Detect unusual activity
